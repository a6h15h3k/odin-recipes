Karan Johar

Description

If you're looking to try a new cuisine, look no further. This is an easy to apetize, culurally appropriated recipe guaranteed to win over the masses. So set aside 6 hours of your life and get read to taste the rainbow of flavours.

Note: It's recommened that you play the listed songs for the dish to come to it's full potential.

This recipe can only be fed through a silver spoon

Ingredients

  • Shahrukh Khan
  • Kajol
  • Johny Lever(JL) for classic comedic flavour
  • Essential Indian Masala
  • Music for emotional manipulation
  • A lot of Cheese-iness

Steps

First Half

  1. Setup an emotional beat that will justify everything we do for the rest of this recipe.
  2. Classic intro song to set the atmosphere
  3. Add Shahrukh and Kajol(SRKJo). Wait for them to caramelize with romance.
  4. Romantic song for the feels
  5. Add a whole bunch of pre-prepped family burden for conflict.
  6. Start preparing a secondary character that will help bring the dish together towards the end. Let’s call this ingredient ‘Rohan’(or any other 90’s name)
  7. Introduction song for Rohan
  8. At this point your pan might be too hot so we need to separate it into 2 pots to bring down the heat.
  9. Emotional song to add some saltiness with those tears.

Interval

  • Due to the length of this recipe, please take a short break and grab some snacks/drinks.

Second Half

  1. Item Song so everyone knows things are not that serious, accompanied with light female objectificaion.
  2. Now that your dish has had some time to cool off, we will add ‘Rohan’ to SRKKJo combo.
  3. Due to the chemical nature, they might not want to mix(this is in fact real science, do not try to think otherwise).
  4. So we will add JL to bring the fluffiness up and add a pinch of secondary conflict to bind them together.
  5. A Conflict Song to speed things up. This will be used later in your workouts.
  6. Please mix the 2 halves together and stir until your able to differentiate what’s what.Your dish is now ready to be shoved down people’s throats as gospel.
  7. An End Credits Song to bring it all together. This will have no relation to what we did for the last 8 1/2 hours of this recipe.
